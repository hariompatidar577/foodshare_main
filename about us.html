About FoodShare

At FoodShare, our mission is simple — to reduce household food waste and help feed communities in need. We believe that no one should go hungry while good food goes to waste.

Our platform connects donors who have surplus food with individuals and organizations that need it. Whether you're a household, a local business, or a non-profit, you can play a role in making food sharing accessible and impactful.

What We Do

  • Enable users to donate leftover or surplus food with ease
  • Connect people in need with available food near them
  • Encourage sustainable food practices in communities

Our Vision

We envision a world where technology helps communities fight hunger and waste — together.
